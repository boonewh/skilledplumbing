Maintenance Tips

Annual Flush

Flush your water heater annually to remove sediment buildup and maintain efficiency.

Temperature Setting

Anode Rod

Signs It's Time to Replace

Rusty Water

Brown or rusty water indicates internal corrosion.

Strange Noises

Rumbling or popping sounds suggest sediment buildup.

Age

Units over 10 years old should be considered for replacement.

Rising Bills

Unexplained increases in energy costs.
